About Me

Hello.  I'm Shobit.  I'm a Master's in Computer Science student at New York University.  I will be graduating in May 2013.

Before I came to New York, I studied engineering (Bachelor's in IT) from University of Mumbai.   On graduating, I worked at Infosys Ltd as a Systems Engineer where my primary responsibility was developing and maintaining our client's web application written in PHP, MySQL and JavaScript.

I love computer science and technology, especially algorithms, databases, programming languages, graphics and the Web.  I enjoy working on large programming projects and building applications.   I primarily code in Java, but I also use C, C++ on some projects, PHP and JavaScript for web applications, and Ruby on Rails at work.

Currently, I'm living in Seattle and working at Amazon as a Software Development Engineer Intern until August 2012.
